\section{Scales}

A scale is a collection of notes in ascending order between a note and its octave. The two main scales are the major (happy sound) and minor (sad sound) scale.

When describing scales, often the terms whole (w) and half (h) steps are used. Moving up a half step on the ukulele means changing moving to the next fret (towards the body).

In terms of notes it means adding a \sharp or going to the next letter.
